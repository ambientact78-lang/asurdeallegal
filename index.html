Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how AssureDeal (“AssureDeal,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application and services (the “Service”).

What AssureDeal Does

AssureDeal helps connect customers with mechanics for services such as Roadside Help (e.g., jump start, tire change, lockout, fuel delivery) and Pre-Purchase Inspections.

Information We Collect

1) Information you provide

  • Request details (service type, notes, car make/model/year, and related info you enter).
  • Address / location description you provide for service or inspection.
  • Basic profile info (such as name or contact details) if you choose to provide it.

2) Location information

  • Precise location (when in use) may be collected if you grant permission, so we can route assistance to you and help mechanics locate the service destination.

3) Push notification data

  • If you opt in to notifications, we may collect a push notification token from your device so we can send updates (e.g., request accepted, ETA updates).

4) Payment information

  • Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers. Stripe may collect and process payment information according to its own policies.

5) Diagnostics (optional)

  • We may collect basic diagnostic information (e.g., app errors) to keep the Service reliable. This does not include the contents of your private notes unless you submit them as part of a request.

How We Use Information

  • To create, display, and manage service requests and inspection requests.
  • To connect customers and mechanics and coordinate job status (accepted/declined/ETA).
  • To process payments via Stripe and help prevent fraud and abuse.
  • To send notifications you request (e.g., job updates).
  • To provide customer support and troubleshoot issues.

How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to operate the Service, including:

  • With mechanics: request details necessary to complete the job (e.g., service type, destination/location, car details, notes relevant to the service).
  • With payment processors: Stripe processes payments for the Service.
  • For legal reasons: if required by law or to protect safety, rights, and integrity of the Service.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention

We keep request records for as long as needed to operate the Service, handle disputes, comply with legal obligations, and improve reliability. You may request deletion as described below.

Your Choices

  • Location: You can disable location permissions in your device settings, but some features may not work.
  • Notifications: You can opt out anytime in your device notification settings.
  • Access / deletion: You can request deletion of your data by contacting us (see “Contact Us”).

Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to help protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Effective date” above when we do.
